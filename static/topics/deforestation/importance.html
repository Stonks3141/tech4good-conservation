Deforestation is the clearing of forests. Although it ocurred mainly in Europe in the past, most deforestation is now in rainforests. Rainforests were the last forests to become accessible for large operations. Slash-and-burn agriculture, mining, and logging are major causes in tropical areas. Since trees produce oxygen, it reduces the Earth's capacity to recover from extra carbon dioxide in the atmosphere. Deforestation is a huge threat to any plant or animal living in a forest. It also contributes to climate change, soil erosion, reduced biodiversity, fewer crops, and flooding.
