In order to conserve plants and animals, it is important to know where they are and how many of them they are. One way to accomplish this is with trackers. They are placed on animals and record their behavior and movements over time. Drones can be used to find and count animals in open terrain using machine learning for image analysis. In wooded or covered areas, trail cameras can be used. These are cameras that turn on and start recording when they detect a warm-bodied animal. Another similar technique is acoustic monitoring. It uses devices that report sounds that they hear.
